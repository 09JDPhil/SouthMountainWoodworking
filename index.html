Deliberate by design

A great experience is never an accident. It's engineered, piece by piece, until it feels effortless. I learned to obsess over the parts no one consciously notices, because those are the parts people feel.

A well built piece outlives whoever made it. That's the standard we hold every piece to: not furniture for a season, but furniture meant to be chosen once, lived with for a generation, and handed down instead of replaced.

That's what you get when you commission a piece from South Mountain Woodworking Company. Furniture built by hand and built to last. Mortise and tenon, dovetails, joinery left visible rather than hidden, because a piece should show you how it was made.

Every piece starts the same way: a conversation about what you need and why it matters to you. If you're ready for that conversation, reach out, I'd love to hear about your project.
